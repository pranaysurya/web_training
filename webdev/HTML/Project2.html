Chemical Symbols

Chemical symbols are a way to define & understand different types of molecules, atoms, & structures at the atomic level.

Chemicals symbols are used to create chemical formulas, which can be used to understand certain processes in day to day life.

Chemical Formulas

Chemical formulas come in all diffent types & combinations, so there are copious amounts of them.

Examples:

H2O (Water)

C6H12O6 (Sugar)

NaCl (Salt)

Cellular Respiration:

Cellular respiration uses oxygen and sugar to create energy. This process is used by all aerobic creatures that need oxygen to survive.

Process:

C6H12O6 + 6 (O2) → ATP + 6 (H2O) + 6 (CO2)

(ATP is basically energy that humans can use, and the water & carbon dioxide is just a by-product of cellular respiration.)

Photosythesis

Photosythesis uses light energy and gases to create energy. This process is used by most plants and certain types of bacteria & fungi

Process:

6(CO2) + 6(H2O) → C6H12O6 + 6(O2).
